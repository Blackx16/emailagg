## Mission

> Build the most reliable, secure, and developer-friendly multi-inbox email aggregation platform — delivered natively through Telegram.

## Vision

EmailAgg is a unified email intelligence platform that lets users centralise emails from Gmail, Outlook, and IMAP accounts into a single Telegram-native interface — with real-time notifications, smart filtering, OTP extraction, and email forwarding — all with zero password sharing.

## Problem Statement

Modern teams manage 3–10+ email accounts across providers. Switching between Gmail, Outlook, and IMAP webclients is slow, fragmented, and error-prone. Critical emails — OTPs, client replies, billing alerts — are missed because there is no unified delivery channel.

## Target Users

- **Agencies** managing multiple client inboxes
- **Startup founders** who live in Telegram
- **Cold outreach operators** running multi-domain campaigns
- **Recruiters** managing high-volume correspondence
- **Freelancers** separating work and personal inboxes
